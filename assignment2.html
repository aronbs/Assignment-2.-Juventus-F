Alessandro Del Piero

Greatest goalscorer

Alessandro Del Piero is Juventus all time leading goalscorer with 290 goals to his name while playing for Juventus from 1993-2012. The Italian wore the jersey number 10, he is considered as the greatest Juventus player.
